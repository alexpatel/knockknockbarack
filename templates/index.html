How does it work?

Knock Knock, Barack' uses order-1 and order-2 Markov models to generate jokes from Obama's political speeches. It constructs Knock-Knock, Yo Mama, and 'Why did the chicken cross the road?' jokes with the help of the part-of-speech tagging functionality of the Natural Language Toolkit.
